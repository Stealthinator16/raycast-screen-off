# Screen Off

A menu bar extension to turn off your display and control caffeinate.

## Features

- **Turn Screen Off**: Instantly turn off your display while keeping your Mac awake (caffeinated)
- **Caffeinate Toggle**: Prevent your Mac from sleeping with a single click
- **Visual Status**: Moon icon shows current state - outline when decaffeinated, filled when caffeinated

## Usage

Click the moon icon in your menu bar to access:

1. **Turn Screen Off** - Turns off the display and enables caffeinate (keeps Mac awake)
2. **Caffeinate / Decaffeinate** - Toggle whether your Mac can sleep

Perfect for:
- Downloading large files overnight with the screen off
- Running long processes without the display on
- Keeping your Mac awake during presentations
